# Falcosend

### High-performance form infrastructure for modern developers.

**Falcosend** is a serverless form backend that bridges the gap between your frontend and your data. No custom APIs, no SMTP configuration—just a powerful, edge-resident endpoint for all your submission needs.

---

## 🧪 Falcosend Lab

The **Falcosend Lab** is our dedicated testing environment. Instead of cluttering your production logs with "test" submissions, use the Lab to validate your integration in real-time.

**Visit the Lab:** [lab.falcosend.ellypad.com](https://lab.falcosend.ellypad.com)

* **Live Submission Testing:** Send real payloads from your local environment to the Lab endpoint to see exactly how they are processed.
* **Instant Feedback:** View real-time logs of incoming requests, including headers, parsed body data, and validation results.
